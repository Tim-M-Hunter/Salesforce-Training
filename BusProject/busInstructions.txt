Here are the requirements for the bus reservation project:

       Store the customer info (name, address, credit card number)

       Store the trip info: reservation #, trip date, bus #, seat #

       Show the customer a seat map

       Allow the customer to change / cancel reservation

       Charge the customer for bags checked

 

Assume there are 2 buses available, each with 3 fares (premium, business, coach):

       Rocket bus: $50, $35, $20 - $7 per bag

       Deluxe bus: $100, $70, $50 - $15 per bag
